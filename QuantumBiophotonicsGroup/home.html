Welcome to the Quantum Biophotonics Group

We use ultrafast spectroscopy, quantum optics, and genetic engineering to unravel the fundamental mechanisms underlying the function of biological molecules and processes.

Quantu​m Biology, Quantum Bioensing and Biophotonics

We investigate the intricate dynamics of electron/proton transfer in chromoprotiens through interactions of light with biomolecular systems, ​deciphering their fundamental role in biological processes. ​The knowledge created from this approach forms the basis for developing innovative photon-based technologies. Through this multidisciplinary approach, we aim to advance our understanding of life's mechanisms and create transformative tools for biology and medicine.
